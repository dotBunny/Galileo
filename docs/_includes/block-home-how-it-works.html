How It Works

Galileo was designed to be simple, yet remarkably effective. The user experience has been tailored for the most optimal workflow with the least amount of input.

Step 1: Locate Your Submissions

You simply need to navigate to the root folder where your paticular assignments submissions are being stored.

Step 2: Start The Process

In the time it takes your coffee machine to heat up and dispense that life-saving fuel, Galileo will process your assignments. You get to remain your cheerful happy self, and Galileo tirelessly analyzes your submitted assignments for anything unsavory. It can save you countless painstaking hours of checking over assignments for violations of your schools academic integrity policies.

Step 3: Read Report

Have a look at the generated report to see if there is anything that stands out as needing further intervention.
